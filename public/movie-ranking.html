The Best Movies According to Ghannam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Worth Of Man

This is my favourite movie. I love the beautiful images.

The Mechanic (2011)

Statham stars as an elite assassin who meticulously plans out his hits. The movie combines action with a well-crafted story and impressive stunts.

The Transporter (2002)

This film features Jason Statham as a highly skilled driver for hire, delivering packages with no questions asked. The action sequences are slick, and Statham's performance is iconic.
